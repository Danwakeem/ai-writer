## Triple homicide in Kansas City parking lot
A shooting in Kansas City, Missouri, left three people dead and five others injured. No arrests have been made and the motive is under investigation. The shooting occurred in a parking lot where a large gathering of people were present. The incident occurred amidst a 28% increase in homicides in the city this year.

## Russia accused of launching deadly airstrike in northwest Syria
Russia has been accused of launching an airstrike on the strategic opposition-held town of Jisr al-Shughur in northwestern Syria, near the Turkish border. The strike left over 30 people wounded, according to the White Helmets civil defense organization, which said the critically injured had died after reaching hospital. Farmers reportedly used bloodied vegetable trucks to transport casualties, with the strike apparently carried out on the town's main vegetable market.
