## Train derailment in Pakistan leaves multiple dead and injured

Rescue teams are working to extract trapped passengers and provide medical assistance. The cause of the accident is under investigation, with theories of mechanical failure or sabotage being explored. This incident highlights the need for improved maintenance and safety standards on Pakistan's railways.
