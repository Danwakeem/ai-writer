## China and Russia strengthen military cooperation amid tensions with the US
China and Russia have reaffirmed their military cooperation, with Chinese Minister of National Defense Li Shangfu expressing the importance of strengthening communication and organizing joint training, patrols, and war games. The military partnership between the two countries demonstrates their informal alliance against the US-led liberal world order. While China has refrained from condemning Russia's invasion of Ukraine, it has provided diplomatic cover for Moscow. Recent developments have positioned Russia as the junior partner in the relationship with China, as the Russian economy heavily relies on energy purchases from China and India. The leaders of China, India, and Russia are scheduled to hold a virtual summit, indicating some support for Russian President Vladimir Putin.

## Hong Kong police issue arrest warrants for pro-democracy activists residing overseas
Hong Kong police have issued arrest warrants for eight pro-democracy activists who are currently living overseas. The activists, including former lawmakers Nathan Law, Ted Hui, and Dennis Kwok, are wanted for violating the National Security Law imposed by Beijing. The police have urged them to return to Hong Kong and surrender, promising a reduction in their sentences. The arrests come as Hong Kong faces increasing scrutiny and crackdowns from Beijing following months of political unrest.

## Bear attacks dog and homeowner in Connecticut
A dog chased a bear inspecting a bird feeder on a porch in Connecticut, resulting in the bear attacking both the dog and the homeowner. The homeowner required hospital treatment, while the dog's condition is unknown. Connecticut experienced a record 67 reports of bears entering homes in 2022, prompting the passing of legislation allowing residents to kill bears in self-defense. Authorities have urged caution and the removal of bird feeders to minimize bear conflicts and damage.

## Shootout at Wichita nightclub leaves multiple wounded
